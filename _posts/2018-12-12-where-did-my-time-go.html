Problem and Idea

I wake up between 5-6am every morning. And I am trying to be as productive as possible over the next 3-4 hours.

But often happens that the morning is over, and I have the feeling that I have done nothing.

In order to prevent this, I started to log every activity that I did on a notepad and the time that I did it in. When the morning was over, I could look at this log and inspect my activities

I realised that I could build a product that simplifies this process a little bit

Design & Build

Design

I wanted to simply log my activities and categorise them as Productive or Unproductive.

Once done, I also wanted to view have a timeline where I could inspect all my activities in a nice and clear manner

Build

I built the product livestreaming it on Twitch. This was a pretty cool experience, but still a bit stressful, as never before people have watched me code.

I would love to continue doing this in the future, but this time with commentary. It would be a good material for YouTube also, as it could show inspiring Software Developers what's coding really like.

Where did my time go was built using ReactJS, as this was the fastest solution for me

Ship

As soon as I hit MVP stage, which took me about 5 hours of programming, I decided to stop working on it and share it to my Twitter account

I have several additional features I plan to include in the near future:

  • Ability to edit Activities
  • Drag and drop activities
  • More statistics in the Timeline page, e.g. Percentage of productive vs unproductive tasks; longest task
